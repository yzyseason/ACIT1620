Bio

Born in Vancouver, BC, in 2002, I was a typical kid captivated by video games, especially Skate 3, which ignited my passion for skateboarding. I also loved playing Halo 3 and Black Ops 2, immersing myself in competitive gaming. After countless hours mastering tricks in my neighborhood, I turned pro with Baker Skateboards, where I not only showcased my skills but also played a crucial role in creating video parts that pushed the brand's creative boundaries.My athletic journey took an exhilarating turn as I also pursued football, inspired by my idols, Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi. In 2025, my dreams came to fruition when I helped Manchester United achieve an incredible treble, winning the Premier League, Champions League, and FA Cup. Now, I navigate the thrill of both skateboarding and football, blending my passions in a unique and exciting way. Recently, I've decided to explore a new interest: web development. I'm diving into coding and design, eager to create my own website to showcase my journey and connect with fans. It's an exciting challenge that complements my athletic pursuits and allows me to express my creativity in a whole new way!
